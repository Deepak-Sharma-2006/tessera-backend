## How to Earn Badges

Each badge has specific requirements. Check BadgeCenter's "Hard-Mode" tab to see your progress.

- Status changes to "Unlocked"

## Key Rules

### 1. Daily Unlock Limit (2 per day)

- Maximum 2 badges can be equipped per 24-hour period
- Limit resets at midnight (00:00)
- Pending badges auto-unlock in order at midnight

### 2. Login Straks

- **Streak Seeker** requires logging in every day
- Missing even 1 day resets streak to 0
- Checked at midnight - if you didn't log in today, streak broken

### 3. Weekly Counters

- **Active Talker (Elite)** counts replies from Monday to Sunday
- Counter resets every Monday at 00:00
- **Weekly Replies**: Track your activity in the user profile

### 4. Maintenance Badges

Some badges need active maintenance:

- **Streak Seeker (Lvl 3)** - Must login daily
- **Profile Perfectionist** - Must keep profile updated every 30 days
- If maintenance lapses, badge auto-removes at midnight

## FAQ

**Q: Can I equip more than 2 badges per day?**
A: No. System enforces 2-badge/day limit. Extra badges go to "pending" and auto-equip tomorrow.

**Q: What happens if I miss a login day?**
A: Your Streak Seeker badge loses progress. Streak resets to 0 at midnight.

**Q: Can I lose a badge?**
A: Yes, maintenance badges (Streak Seeker, Profile Perfectionist) auto-remove if criteria lapse.

**Q: Do older badges matter?**
A: Yes, they count toward "Voice of the Hub" (1500 total) and other cumulative badges.

**Q: What's the fastest badge to earn?**
A: **First Responder** - just reply first to one post. Then fastest others take hours of activity.

**Q: What's the hardest badge?**
A: **Voice of the Hub (Lvl 3)** - requires 1500 total replies (massive time investment).

**Q: Can multiple people earn the same badge?**
A: Yes! Each user can earn all 20 badges independently.

**Q: What if I earn a badge at 11:50 PM?**
A: It's badge #1. You have until midnight to equip badge #2, or it'll wait until next day.
